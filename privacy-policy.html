Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 26, 2023

This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide to us when you visit our website or use our services.

Information We Collect:

When you create an account, we may ask you to provide certain personal information, such as your name and email address. This information is used solely to provide you with a personalized experience on our website and to contact you regarding the services you use.

Use of Information:

We do not store IP addresses. The information you share with us is only used for the purposes for which you provided it. This includes managing your account, delivering the requested services, and providing customer support.

Sharing Information:

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, even as described above or with your consent. However, the data is stored in Firebase by Google, the login provider.

Security:

We take reasonable measures to ensure the security of your personal information. We strive to protect your data from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

Changes to This Privacy Policy:

This privacy policy may be updated to reflect changes in our practices. We recommend checking this page regularly for the latest information about our privacy policy.
