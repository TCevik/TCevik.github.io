With your permission, we may use your personal information to send you newsletters, marketing or promotional materials, and other information that may be of interest to you. You can opt out of this communication at any time by following the instructions in the respective emails.

We only retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes mentioned above, unless a longer retention period is legally required or permitted.

Our website uses cookies to store certain information and remember when you visit our site. Cookies enable us to remember your preferences and provide you with a personalized experience. You can adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies, but this may affect the functionality of our website.
